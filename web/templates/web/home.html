What is RoomieMatch?

RoomieMatch is a platform that helps university students find their ideal roommates. Whether you're looking for someone who shares your study habits or enjoys the same hobbies, RoomieMatch makes roommate selection easy and stress-free.

Our matching algorithm ensures that you're connected with students who align with your preferences, course, and interests. Say goodbye to mismatched roommates and hello to harmonious living arrangements!
